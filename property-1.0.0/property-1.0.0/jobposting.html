Job Title: Telecaller

Preferred: Female

Experience: Fresher - 2 years

Job Description

We are looking for a motivated Telecaller to become part of our team.In this role, you will connect with potential clients and be a crucial part of our customer engagement process. Your primary responsibility will be to provide information about our services, address inquiries, and guide clients through their initial interactions with Samprapthi Properties.

Responsibilities:

  • Make and receive calls, providing clients with information about our offerings.
  • Address customer questions and concerns with clarity and professionalism.
  • Keep the customer database up to date with accurate and relevant information.
  • Coordinate with the sales team to ensure smooth client management.
  • Arrange appointments and follow up with prospective clients as needed.

Skills Required:

  • Strong verbal communication skills and a friendly demeanor.
  • Ability to manage and resolve customer inquiries effectively.
  • Basic computer literacy and familiarity with CRM tools.

Application Process:

Interested candidates should send their resume and a brief cover letter to hr@samprapthi.com. Please include 'Telecaller Application' in the subject line. Our hiring team will review your submission and reach out if your profile matches our requirements.

Job Title: BDM (Business Development Manager)

Preferred: Female

Experience: 3 - 7 years

Job Description

We are in search of a skilled Business Development Manager (BDM) who will be instrumental in expanding our business. The ideal candidate should have excellent client interaction skills, experience in conducting site visits, and fluency in English. Your role will involve identifying potential business opportunities, nurturing client relationships, and ensuring that our services meet client expectations.

Responsibilities

  • Identify and pursue new business prospects within the real estate market.
  • Build and maintain positive relationships with clients and key stakeholders.
  • Conduct on-site visits and engage in client meetings to understand their needs.
  • Develop and present tailored proposals to potential clients.
  • Collaborate with the sales and marketing teams to formulate growth strategies.

Skills Required:

  • Fluent in English with strong communication skills, both written and spoken.
